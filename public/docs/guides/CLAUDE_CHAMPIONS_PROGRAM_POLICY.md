# Claude Champions Program Policy
**Enterprise AI Deployment | Effective December 2025**

---

## Program Overview
The Claude Champions Program empowers early adopters to drive AI adoption, provide peer support, and shape Claude's integration across the organization.

---

## Champion Responsibilities

**Core Duties** (4-6 hours/month)
- **Advocate**: Promote Claude adoption within your department through demos and use case sharing
- **Support**: Provide first-line assistance to colleagues (basic troubleshooting, prompt guidance)
- **Feedback**: Submit monthly insights on usage patterns, feature requests, and pain points
- **Training**: Complete quarterly champion training sessions and share learnings with teams

**Eligibility**: Active Claude user for 30+ days, manager approval, commitment to 6-month term

---

## Support & Resources

**What Champions Receive**
- Priority access to new Claude features (beta testing opportunities)
- Monthly office hours with AI enablement team (1st Tuesday, 2:00 PM ET)
- Dedicated Slack channel: `#claude-champions` for peer collaboration
- Champion toolkit: Templates, presentation decks, FAQ database

**What Champions Should NOT Do**
- Handle security incidents (escalate immediately)
- Make promises about feature delivery timelines
- Share confidential roadmap information externally

---

## Escalation Process

| Issue Type | Action | Response Time |
|------------|--------|---------------|
| **General questions** | Respond directly or consult champion channel | 24 hours |
| **Technical bugs** | Document and escalate to `#claude-support` | 48 hours |
| **Security concerns** | Immediately escalate to `security@company.com` | 15 minutes |
| **Account/billing issues** | Route to IT service desk ticket | 24 hours |

## Recognition & Rewards

**Quarterly Awards**
- Top Contributor Award: $500 gift card + executive recognition
- Innovation Award: Best new use case implemented ($250 gift card)
- All active champions: Certificate of achievement, LinkedIn endorsement

**Career Benefits**
- "AI Champion" credential on internal profile
- Priority consideration for AI-related projects and roles
- Professional development hours counted toward annual learning goals

**Annual Summit**: Top 10 champions invited to company AI strategy retreat (all expenses paid)

---

## Program Governance
- **Program Manager**: AI Enablement Team Lead
- **Review Cycle**: Quarterly performance check-ins
- **Opt-Out**: 30-day notice required; no penalties for voluntary exit
- **Removal**: Automatic for policy violations or 2+ consecutive months of inactivity

*Questions? Contact: `ai-champions@company.com`*
